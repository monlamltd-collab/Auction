Auction can be a preferred method of sale due to the certainty for vendor and purchaser, with contracts deemed to have exchanged when the auction ends. Where competitive bidding occurs, guide prices can also often be exceeded at auction.

As part of our sales service, we work with clients to sell their assets - farms and land as well as residential and commercial property and development assets - through online property auction.
