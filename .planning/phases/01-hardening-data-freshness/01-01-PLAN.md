# Plan 01-01: SDLT Calculator Fix — England, Scotland & Wales

<objective>
Fix the SDLT calculator to correctly handle all three UK jurisdictions for investment property purchases. England already uses the correct 5% surcharge bands but Scotland (LBTT + 6% ADS) and Wales (LTT + 4% higher rate) have no implementation at all. Both `index.html` and `bridgematch-lite.html` must be updated in sync.
</objective>

<must_haves>
- calcSDLT() accepts a country/region parameter and returns correct investor stamp duty for England, Scotland, and Wales
- Scotland LBTT + 6% ADS calculation matches Revenue Scotland published rates
- Wales LTT + 4% higher rate calculation matches Welsh Revenue Authority published rates
- Both index.html and bridgematch-lite.html use identical calculation logic
- calcDealAnalysis() in index.html passes the country parameter through to calcSDLT()
</must_haves>

<tasks>

<task id="01-01-01" title="Refactor calcSDLT() in index.html to accept country parameter">
<action>
In `index.html` at ~line 2460, refactor `calcSDLT(price)` to `calcSDLT(price, country)` where country defaults to `'england'`.

Keep existing England bands as-is (they are correct for 2025/26 investor rates with 5% surcharge):
- 0-250k: 5%
- 250k-925k: 10%
- 925k-1.5M: 15%
- 1.5M+: 17%

Add Scotland LBTT + ADS (6% Additional Dwelling Supplement on full price, plus LBTT bands):
- ADS: 6% on full purchase price
- LBTT bands: 0-145k: 0%, 145k-250k: 2%, 250k-325k: 5%, 325k-750k: 10%, 750k+: 12%
- Total = ADS amount + LBTT amount

Add Wales LTT higher rates (replace standard rates, not additive):
- 0-180k: 4%
- 180k-250k: 7.5%
- 250k-400k: 9%
- 400k-750k: 11.5%
- 750k-1.5M: 14%
- 1.5M+: 16%

Use a switch/case on `country` parameter ('england', 'scotland', 'wales') with 'england' as default.
</action>
