**Fix 2 -- Move catch-all route (line 4623-4635):**
The `app.get('*', ...)` catch-all at line 4623 is registered BEFORE three admin GET routes at lines 11094, 11122, and 11176. Express matches routes in registration order, so the catch-all intercepts `/api/admin/alerts`, `/api/admin/freshness`, and `/api/admin/analytics`, returning HTML instead of JSON.

Fix: Cut the entire catch-all block (lines 4623-4635, including the closing `});`) from its current position. Paste it immediately after line 11230 (after the last route registration `/api/track/event`), before the `// ACTIVITY LOGGING & STATS` section. Add a comment: `// Catch-all: serve index.html for client-side routing (MUST be after all API routes)`.

Do NOT change the catch-all logic itself -- only its position in the file.
